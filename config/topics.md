#Remote Sensing of Peatlands
Research on remote sensing peatlands including peatland health. Involving techniques such as hyperspectral reflectance, SAR, foundational models. Specific focus on Sphagnum.

#Sphagnum moss remote sensing
Research linking physiology and stress in Sphagnum mosses with remote sensing, or spectral characteristics. 

#Ground truthing remote sensing of plants.
Research that ground truths remote sensing of plants and ecosystems under stress. Including forests, peatlands. 

#The effect of elevated CO2 on ecosystems
The effect of elevated CO2 on plant ecosystems. Particularly with links to remote sensing. 

#Understanding remote sensing and machine learning
Research which links remote sensing of plants and ecosystems to biological processes. Also biological interpretation of machine learning and foundational models in remote sensing of forests and peatlands and other ecosystems.
